Franka meshes are from:
https://github.com/frankaemika/franka_ros/tree/develop/franka_description/meshes

franka.urdf is generated using ROS xacro macros, franka_*.urdf are modified from
it to accommodate specifi use cases (dual-arm with franka_dual.urdf,
non-prehensile manipulation with franka_small_fingers.urdf)
